Session Overview

Meaningful Moments Map

A personal strengths discovery experience where participants uncover the pattern running through their proudest moments, name their superpower, and connect it to how they show up at work and with their team.

The Experience

Superpower insights that shine light on who you are and the gifts you bring.

Participants map their proudest moments across three chapters of their life, look for the pattern, and name the unique quality that's been with them all along. Then in small groups, they share their discoveries, see each other's strengths, and explore how their combined superpowers could collide and become even greater.

It's personal development grounded in real experience, not a quiz. And the insights come from the participants themselves, not a facilitator telling them who they are.

How It Flows

01

Arrive + Connect

Small group warm-up. Participants settle in and connect with the people near them through a quick, positive prompt.

02

Map Your Moments

Each person divides a large piece of paper into three life chapters and maps their proudest moments across each one. Quiet reflection with background music.

03

Find the Pattern

Guided prompts help participants look across their moments and name the skill, quality, or "superpower DNA" that runs through all of them.

04

Share + Discover Together

In groups of 3, participants share their maps and superpowers. Peers affirm what they see, compare strengths, and explore how their combined powers could create something greater.

05

Reflect + Commit

Whole group reflection. Each participant commits to one way they'll bring more of their superpower into their work this quarter.

What Participants Walk Away With

✓

A named personal superpower grounded in their own real experiences, not assigned by a test

✓

A visual map of their proudest moments across their life that they can keep

✓

Deeper appreciation for the unique strengths their teammates bring

✓

A concrete commitment to bring more of their superpower into their daily work

✓

Real connection with colleagues through sharing something meaningful (not surface-level small talk)

The Aha Moment

The superpower was there all along, across every life chapter. Participants don't get assigned a type from a quiz. They discover the pattern themselves, name it, and see how to bring more of it to work.

Two Session Styles

Choose the version that fits your energy.

Both versions follow the same core arc. The difference is how the session closes.

Recommended

Option A: Reflective Arc

The full 90 minutes focused on personal discovery, peer sharing, and whole-group reflection. Spacious. Every moment has room to breathe. Ends with a quiet commitment and group insights.

Vibe: Reflective, warm, spacious
Closes with: Whole-group reflection and personal commitment
Materials: Large paper, markers, colored pencils
Best if: Your team is coming from heavier strategy sessions and needs breathing room

Option B: Creative Breakout

Same discovery arc, slightly compressed to make room for a hands-on creative extension. Groups build a collaborative "Super Creation" representing their combined superpowers using art supplies, then present it gallery-walk style. Higher energy. Produces a physical artifact.

Vibe: Energetic, playful, memorable
Closes with: Gallery walk presentations and team photos
Materials: Paper, markers, plus clay, craft supplies, Polaroid camera
Best if: You want something the team will photograph, talk about, and remember at dinner

Why This Works

Gallup research shows that people who use their strengths daily are 6x more likely to be engaged at work, 3x more likely to report excellent quality of life, and 15% less likely to leave their company. But most people can't name their strengths because they've never been asked to look for the pattern across their whole life, not just their current role. This session gives them that opportunity.

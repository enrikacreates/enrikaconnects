Can we be honest?

We're more connected than ever...
and lonelier than we've been in decades.

Our teams meet on screens. Our gatherings stay surface-level. We've optimized every hour for productivity and forgotten how to simply be together. Somewhere along the way, we stopped playing. We stopped making things with our hands. We stopped telling each other real stories.

And we're feeling it. In our teams, our communities, and ourselves. The burnout. The disengagement. The quiet sense that something essential is missing.

What we're actually craving

We want rooms where people actually see each other.

We want permission to move, to laugh, to be a little bit awkward together... and discover that's where the real connection lives. We want to make something with our hands and walk away surprised by what we're capable of. We want to tell our stories and hear someone say, "me too."

We don't need another lecture. We need an experience that reminds us we're human.

Move. Connect. Play. Create.

Four doorways back to what matters most.

Here's what happens when I'm in the room

I don't do lectures.

I create interactive, hands-on experiences where people move their bodies, use their hands, tell real stories, and leave different than when they walked in.

Every workshop is designed around your group, your goals, and the outcome you're after. Whether it's a leadership team that needs to rebuild trust, a conference audience that's been sitting too long, or a retreat group ready to go deeper. I meet people where they are and create the conditions for something real to happen.

Your attendees won't just feel inspired. They'll leave having moved their bodies, told a real story, made something with their hands, and walked away with something they'll actually do after the workshop is over.

What I Offer

Four Ways to Bring This
to Your Event

Move

Embodied Energy & Activation

We spend most of our days from the neck up. Thinking, planning, scrolling, sitting. MOVE brings people back into their bodies. Participants start with a simple shake-out, move into a light stretch, and before they know it, they're a little out of breath, smiling, laughing, and remembering they are full-bodied beings. Not just brains in chairs.

Best for: Conference openers, midday re-energizers, retreat warm-ups, pairing with any session

Connect

Storytelling & Human Connection

We've forgotten how to tell our own stories. And how to really listen to someone else's. In these experiences, participants practice the lost art of personal storytelling, deep listening, and showing up authentically. Teams build the kind of trust that no icebreaker can manufacture.

Best for: Leadership retreats, team building, community gatherings, faith communities

Play

Rediscovering Play as a Growth Tool

What if the key to unlocking creativity, resilience, and joy wasn't more discipline... but more play? These workshops are science-backed, surprisingly profound, and genuinely fun. Participants discover how play supercharges both personal and professional growth, and they'll never see "frivolous fun" the same way again.

Best for: Corporate workshops, conferences, women's retreats, team offsites

Create

Hands-On Creative Making

There's something that happens when people make things together that conversation alone can't touch. These sessions get participants out of their heads and into their hands. Painting, building, crafting, writing. Unlocking creative expression as a pathway to connection, confidence, and hope.

Best for: Retreats, offsites, creative communities, women's gatherings

Every experience is customized to your group. These four themes can stand alone, be combined, or be adapted to fit your event's specific goals. MOVE pairs especially well as an opener for any session.

Meet Enrika

Connection is the way of love.
Play is the pathway to joy.
Creativity is a catalyst for hope.

For over a decade, I've been leading workshops, coaching creatives, and helping people rediscover the wisdom they already carry within. As a Head Coach at Seth Godin's altMBA, the Chief Community Advocate for The Story Republic, and the Chief Storyteller at Small Gorilla Creative, I've learned the best ways to help brands tell stories that actually matter.

I'm also a Certified Dream Big Coach through Bob Goff's Dream Big Academy and a guest Guide at The Oaks Retreat Center in San Diego, where I facilitate immersive experiences for groups seeking deeper connection.

When I'm not in a room full of people I love, you'll find me making things with my hands (leather bags, jewelry, screen prints) or playing guitar on my patio. I'm an artist, a maker, and a storyteller who believes the most important things happen when we slow down enough to really be with each other.
